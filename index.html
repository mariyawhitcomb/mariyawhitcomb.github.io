About me

Hi! My name is Mariya Whitcomb. I was born and raised in Russia and moved to the United Stated after graduating from Siberian Academy of Government Service.While studying Information Technologies in Government Management I have mastered skills that allow me to communicate effectively, make educated and well thought out decisions and find resourceful solutions to challenging problems.

A few years ago I have decided to enter into educational field and became a teacher. I have used my communication skills, attention to details, creativity and motivation, along with positive attitude and ability to work well in a team to ensure that all students achieve developmental goals and establish consistent daily communication between parents, teachers and school administration.

Although teaching is extremely rewarding after much consideration I have decided to pursue my true passion and become a Web Developer. As technology is rapidly growing I am thrilled to be a part of this industry and be able to use the tools to make a positive impact on my local and global community. During my time at GA I went through 500+ hours of training in the most in-demand software skills. I now am confident this is the right choice for me and I am beyond excited to start implementing my skills and open new possibilities for achieving my goals.
